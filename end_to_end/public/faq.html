    Frequently Asked Questions

    RAGBot is an intelligent document assistant that uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology. It works by analyzing your uploaded PDF documents, understanding their content, and allowing you to ask questions about the information they contain. The system combines advanced document retrieval with natural language processing to provide accurate, context-aware answers directly from your documents.
    RAGBot offers several key benefits: • Quick information extraction from lengthy documents • Accurate answers based directly on your document content • Time-saving alternative to manual document searching • Secure and private document processing • Simple and intuitive chat interface • Support for multiple document types
    Currently, RAGBot supports PDF files up to 10MB in size. We process the text content of these documents while maintaining their original formatting and structure. Future updates will include support for additional file formats such as DOC, DOCX, and TXT.
    We take data security seriously. Your documents are processed securely on our servers and are only used to answer your specific questions. We do not store the original documents permanently, and all processed data is encrypted. The system operates on a per-session basis, meaning your document data is cleared once you're done using the service.
    RAGBot's responses are highly accurate because they are directly based on the content of your documents. The system uses advanced AI to understand context and generate relevant answers. However, like any AI system, it works best when questions are clear and specific. The accuracy also depends on the quality and clarity of the uploaded documents.
    Yes, there are some basic limits to ensure optimal performance: • Maximum file size: 10MB per PDF • Supported format: PDF files only (currently) • Query rate: Standard rate limiting applies to prevent system overload • Session duration: Automatic cleanup after extended inactivity
