    Frequently Asked Questions

    A RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) chatbot is an advanced type of AI assistant that combines natural language generation with document retrieval. It fetches relevant information from a knowledge base or database and uses that to generate more accurate and context-aware responses.
    When user asks a question, the chatbot searches through a collection of documents using similarity matching. It retrieves the most relevant pieces of text, then uses a language model to generate a response based on that retrieved content.
    The chatbot can work with PDFs. As long as the documents are indexed properly during setup, the chatbot can retrieve information from them to answer questions.
    The chatbot's knowledge depends on the documents it retrieves from. To keep it up to date, new or revised documents need to be added and re-indexed.
    The chatbot is best at answering questions based on the provided documents.
