    • About Us

    Welcome to RAGBot, your intelligent document assistant powered by advanced AI technology. Our platform combines the power of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with an intuitive chat interface to help you extract insights from your documents effortlessly. Whether you're analyzing research papers, technical documentation, or business reports, RAGBot provides accurate, context-aware responses based on your uploaded documents.

    Our mission is to make document interaction more efficient and intuitive. By leveraging state-of-the-art natural language processing, we ensure that you can quickly find the information you need without having to manually search through lengthy documents.
